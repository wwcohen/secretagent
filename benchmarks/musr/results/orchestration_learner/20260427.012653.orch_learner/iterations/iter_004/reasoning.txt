2. **How many failures share this pattern:** The vast majority of the 18 failures shown (e.g., ex063, ex060, ex022, ex083) feature the LLM choosing the suspect with a generic weapon connection over the suspect with the exact/specific connection.
3. **Minimal change that fixes this pattern:** I am updating the docstrings for `extract_suspects_and_evidence`, `verify_alibis`, and `deduce_murderer` to explicitly extract and analyze "specific clues" and "exact weapon matches", prompting the LLM to search for the definitive "Smoking Gun" that breaks the tie between two otherwise viable suspects.
4. **Why this change will NOT break currently working cases:** It perfectly preserves the Chain-of-Thought ("Think step-by-step") format and exact string output formatting ("Final Answer: ...") that proved successful in Iteration 3. It merely sharpens the heuristics the LLM uses during that thought process to prevent it from getting distracted by red herrings.
