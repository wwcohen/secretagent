To safely unlock the 84% "thinking" accuracy without breaking working cases, we need to prompt for holistic step-by-step reasoning but pair it with a **bulletproof parsing logic** that perfectly isolates the final conclusion.

### Changes Made:
1. **Prompt Augmentation:** Appended a natural CoT prompt to the question passed to `raw_answer` asking it to analyze constraints/dynamics and explicitly conclude with `"FINAL CHOICE: <exact text>"`. This avoids restrictive formatting rules that confuse the LLM.
2. **Robust Multi-stage Extraction:** 
   - First, safely searches for the `FINAL CHOICE:` marker to extract the exact text or choice number.
   - Second, if the marker is slightly malformed, we use `.rfind()` to find the *last* mentioned choice string in the text (ensuring we capture the conclusion of the thought process, not the setup).
   - Finally, we retain `extract_index` as the ultimate fallback, ensuring zero risk of extraction crashes.
