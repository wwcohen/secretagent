2. **Impacted cases:** Almost every failure case highlighted (776, 895, 922, 750, 809, 49) suffers from ignored negative constraints. 
3. **Minimal change:** Add a `CRITICAL - NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS` section to the `parse_schedules` docstring, giving the LLM precise instructions on mapping these constraints to standard 24-hour busy blocks (e.g., `["00:00", "23:59"]` for a full day). Additionally, add a small hint to `find_available_slots` to strictly enforce intersection checks.
4. **Why it won't break working cases:** It maintains the exact same JSON schema and pipeline architecture. For cases without negative constraints, the behavior remains identical. For cases with them, the `schedules` dict will accurately reflect the unavailable time, organically preventing `find_available_slots` from scheduling meetings during those periods.
