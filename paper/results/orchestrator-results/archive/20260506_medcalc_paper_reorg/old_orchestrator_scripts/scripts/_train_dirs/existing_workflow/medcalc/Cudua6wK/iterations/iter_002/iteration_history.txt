1. **Date & Lab Value Extraction Failures:** Non-scoring calculators (e.g., Estimated Due Date, Date of Conception, Free Water Deficit, Calcium Correction) fail because the LLM struggles with date arithmetic, specific unit mapping, and isolating exactly which number to extract when multiple are present. 2. **Over-Inferring Scoring Conditions:** For scoring systems (CHA2DS2-VASc, Wells, PERC), the LLM is aggressively hallucinating conditions based on the prompt instructing it to find conditions "IMPLIED by clinical findings" (e.g. extracting Heart Failure just because an EF% was mentioned somewhere, or mapping a past resolved event to the current presentation).
